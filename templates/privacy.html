Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information you provide at registration (name, email address, password).
  • Expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.
  • Basic usage data such as login timestamps and session activity.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and reports.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app stability.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, your personal data and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may use limited third-party infrastructure (such as hosting providers) solely to operate the service. These providers are contractually restricted from using your data for any other purpose.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app. We will respond to all reasonable requests within a reasonable timeframe.
